Naming conventions for Events:
	* All CamelCase

		Items
		* info: CamelCase item received
		* subtype: "Get"
		* type: "Item"

	PositionChange
		* info: str(newPlace)

	Lap
		* info: str(newLap)
		* subtype: "Few" 

	Finish
		* info: str(place finished in) 
		* subtype: "Finish"

	Shortcut
		* type: "Shortcut"
		* subtype: "Shortcut"
		* info: str(Which Shortcut?)
